Home

Scale Tabs allows you to organize your multiple tabs, finding exactly the tab your looking for at any time.

All you need to do is to click on the Scalle!!! icon on your status bar and select the tab which you want to go for.

The most important functionality of this Jetpack is the filter capability, which allow you to find the tabs that you are looking for based on keywords passed as input to it.

How to use

In order to use scale your Firefox tabs you need to:

  • Install the Scale Tabs plugin on your Firefox browser.
  • Click on the Escale!!! link available on your Firefox status bar.
  • Input some text to filter the possibilies of choices among the tabs, accordingly to their name.
  • Click on the displayed tab which you wish to choose.
