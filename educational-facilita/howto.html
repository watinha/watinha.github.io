How to use

Well... it is pretty simple to use Educational Facilita:

  • First, click on the orange link called Educational on the status bar of your Firefox. Try to apply Educational Facilita to Webpages that contain lots of textual informations and are written in Portuguese. Some examples of websites in which you should try Educational Facilita: UOL and Terra.
  • Wait for the textual processing operations be complete. You will be notified on a message displayed on the top right of your desktop and the tab in which you activated the Educational Facilita will also be prompted in orange color, when the operations complete. Be aware that the operations might take more than 60 seconds until they complete, so go grab a cup of nice tea while you wait.
  • Finally, just look on the orange highlighted links that appeared over the text of the page that you were visiting. When you click on these links you will be presented with easier synonymous or additional informations about that text element.
